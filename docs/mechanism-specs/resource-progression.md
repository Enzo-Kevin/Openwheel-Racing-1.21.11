# Resource Progression Mechanism Spec

## Purpose

- What is the intended survival progression from vanilla Minecraft into racing?

  Mid-phase mostly, just for fun now

- Which racing resources should be early-game, mid-game, and late-game?

  crude oil can start from early-game and build small cars (karting) that can run anywhere, mid-game track will start to build and start to time trail, late-game the car will evolve into f1.

- Should players be able to build a basic car before finding specialized resources?

  Yes of course, and I think we might need to specify car build models to distinguish karts, F4, F2 and F1

## Progression Questions

- What should the first 30 minutes of survival racing progression look like?

  Mining crude oil and refining into rubber for building their first kart for normal transportation

- What should require mining?

  Most of the things, because you must get crude oil before anything starts

- What should require crafting stations?

  Car building and basically most of the things

- What should require world exploration?

  Carbon fiber because it required netherite-similar stuff

- What should be available in villages, loot, or trades?

  crude oil and carbon fiber

- What resources should be renewable?

  Rubber renewed from rubber-made stuff without loss, electronics decomposed without loss
